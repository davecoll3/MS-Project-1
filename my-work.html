My Work

PAPIER

PAPIER is a fanzine created and edited by Jennie Zakrewski. It has been distributed in Metz since 2019. For each edition, she reflects and digresses, with poetry and disillusion, around a theme of her choosing. She invites an illustrator for each edition and they are free to reimagine her texts as images. I was invited to participate in the fifth edition, dedicated to animals, which is live and available online. All the illustrations were created using Procreate.
